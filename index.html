[outer veil] is a San Francisco-based record label devoted to unearthing terrestrial, cross-genre sounds. Situated in the hazy penumbra of the North American club ecosystem, it constructs low-lit environments where distinct works emerge from the interstices of psychedelic and experimental. by Oso Feo, kk.Shucko, fallen matter, 2026

A haven glows in the understory, a pool thrums beneath the foliage. Harboured on the water's brim, unseen organisms enter a cyclical, catabolic dance. In this sunless refuge, they feast on death and emanate light. Come, look: life remakes itself from its detritus. Under the veil, we unearth a morphology in which the new germinates from the old, in shrouded conditions hospitable to peculiarity and intrigue. Equal parts terrestrial and cybernetic, this volume showcases a breadth of creations emerging from the North American club ecosystem. After the long shelter, comes Spring.
